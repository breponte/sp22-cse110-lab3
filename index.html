Agenda

Today's Mission: Brainstorming for Magic 8 Ball Project. The objective is to generate at least 10 ideas for features. In the next meeting we will expand on our ideas from this meeting by merging those ideas. Focus on breadth not depth of ideas! Several bad ideas are better than one good idea. Do not spend too much time on one idea.

Prior Meeting Details

Last Meeting's Mission: Delegation of roles for Magic 8 Ball Project, Establish team culture expectations. The objective was to split our group into smaller partners/teams in order to reduce the bus factor while also working on the entire project as a whole. Another goal was to establish rules and expectations of interaction within the group, for partners and between the teams. Team adjustments will not occur unless catastrophic consequences require so.

Current Meeting Details

Assigning members that were not able to make it last meeting.

Comments, Questions, Concerns

  • What if my partner and I do not get along?
    ANSWER: Notify your partner of the current dynamic between you two and attempt to brainstorm solutions and causes that led to the situation. If this is not possible then notify team leads so that they may be able to come to a consensus by promoting better team culture. If the situation becomes difficult, measures such as team changes or TA involvement may be necessary depending on the deduced causes.

  • What will be our time expectations? I tend to be very busy.
    ANSWER: Unfortunately, we do not know. This project, as all project typically are, has a great deal of uncertainty.
